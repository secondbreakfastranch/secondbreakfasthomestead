About the Homestead

Second Breakfast Homestead is a working regenerative farm. We raise pastured livestock, grow plant starters and perennials, and breed ornamental fish in integrated outdoor pond systems. Everything we do is connected: animals build soil, soil grows plants, plants filter water, water sustains fish.

We don’t do this because it’s trendy. We do it because it works. Regenerative agriculture produces better food, healthier land, and more resilient systems than the industrial alternatives. We’re here to prove that — and to share what we learn along the way.

What You’ll Find Here

Honest information. Articles, guides, and resources written from direct experience. If something failed, we’ll say so. If we’re still figuring it out, you’ll know.

Practical methods. Nothing here requires a degree or a trust fund. The techniques we use work on five acres or a backyard. They’re designed to be replicated, adapted, and improved.

Real products. When we recommend gear, supplies, or livestock equipment, it’s because we’ve used it. Some links are affiliate links (see our privacy policy for details) — they cost you nothing and help keep this site running.
