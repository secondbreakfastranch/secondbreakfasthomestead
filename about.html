About the Homestead

50|

Three operations, one system. A working farm built on regenerative practice.

51|
52| 53|
54| 55|

Second Breakfast Homestead is a working regenerative farm. We raise pastured 56| livestock, grow plant starters and perennials, and breed ornamental fish in 57| integrated outdoor pond systems. Everything we do is connected: animals build 58| soil, soil grows plants, plants filter water, water sustains fish.

59| 60|

We don't do this because it's trendy. We do it because regenerative 61| agriculture produces better food, healthier land, and more resilient systems 62| than the industrial alternatives. We're here to prove that — and to share 63| what we learn along the way.
