About the Homestead

Three operations, one system. A working farm built on regenerative practice.

Second Breakfast Homestead is a working regenerative farm. We raise pastured livestock, grow plant starters and perennials, and breed ornamental fish in integrated outdoor pond systems. Everything we do is connected: animals build soil, soil grows plants, plants filter water, water sustains fish.

We don't do this because it's trendy. We do it because regenerative agriculture produces better food, healthier land, and more resilient systems than the industrial alternatives. We're here to prove that — and to share what we learn along the way.

What We Offer

Livestock & Eggs. Pasture-raised pork and eggs from animals raised on rotated pasture and forest understory. No concrete, no confinement. Browse livestock & eggs →

Garden & Perennials. Vegetable starters, fruit and nut trees, pond plants, and soil-building perennials — all started in living soil and ready for real conditions. Browse garden & perennials →
