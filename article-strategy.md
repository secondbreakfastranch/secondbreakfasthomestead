# Second Breakfast Homestead — Article Strategy

## Content Philosophy

Every article answers a real question someone would type into Google. No
"what is regenerative agriculture" word-salad. Specific, useful, written from
direct experience. The reader should leave with something they can do.

Target: 1,000–1,400 words per article. Affiliate links only where genuinely
relevant — product recommendations that make the advice actionable.

## Tier 1: Foundation Articles (Ship First — 9 articles)

These establish the three pillars and cover the core topics. Publish 3 at
launch (one per pillar), then 1 per week via AutoPublisher.

### Livestock
1. **Pastured Pork 101: Why Forest-Raised Beats Factory-Farmed**
   Covers: breed selection, pasture rotation, shelter, processing timeline.
   Affiliate: portable electric fencing, waterers, processing equipment.

2. **Backyard Eggs: Setup, Breed Choice, and What Nobody Tells You**
   Covers: coop design, feed vs. forage, winter laying, predator protection.
   Affiliate: chicken coops, feeders, waterers, nesting boxes.

3. **Processing Your Own Meat: A Practical Guide**
   Covers: tools, space requirements, timing, common mistakes.
   Affiliate: processing kits, vacuum sealers, butcher paper.

### Plants
4. **Starting Plants in Living Soil: Better Than the Nursery**
   Covers: soil blocking, seed starting mix, light, hardening off.
   Affiliate: soil blockers, grow lights, heat mats, seed trays.

5. **Fruit Trees for the Homestead: What to Plant and Why**
   Covers: rootstock choice, planting, first-year care, variety by zone.
   Affiliate: bare-root trees, tree guards, planting amendments.

6. **Comfrey: The Plant That Pays for Itself**
   Covers: propagation, planting, harvesting, uses (mulch, feed, compost).
   Affiliate: root cuttings, garden forks, compost thermometers.

### Aquatics
7. **Medaka (Japanese Rice Fish): The Perfect Outdoor Aquarium Fish**
   Covers: tub setup, breeding, overwintering, why they're ideal.
   Affiliate: outdoor tubs, spawning mops, brine shrimp hatcheries.

8. **Building a Pond That Cleans Itself**
   Covers: sizing, plants, filtration, fish load, seasonal maintenance.
   Affiliate: pond liners, pumps, filters, test kits.

9. **Aquascaping With Purpose: Design for Living Systems**
   Covers: hardscape, plant selection, fish compatibility, cycling.
   Affiliate: substrate, driftwood, lighting, CO2 systems.

## Tier 2: Expansion (Week 5–8 — 4 articles)

Deeper dives and cross-pillar topics.

10. **Chickens Following Pigs: How Rotational Grazing Builds Soil**
    Cross-pillar: livestock + plants. Practical integration guide.

11. **Pond Plants That Filter Water (And Look Good Doing It)**
    Cross-pillar: plants + aquatics. Bog filters, marginals, floaters.

12. **Homestead Water Systems: From Rain Barrel to Pond**
    Infrastructure article. Capture, storage, distribution.

13. **Raising Guppies Outdoors: Summer Color, Winter Strategy**
    Seasonal aquatic article. Breeder tubs, line management.

## Tier 3: Evergreen & Seasonal (Ongoing)

- Seasonal planting calendars
- Winter homestead prep checklists
- Breeder reports (specific fish spawns, livestock outcomes)
- Equipment reviews (tested, not affiliate baited)

## Publishing Cadence

- **Launch:** 3 articles (1 per pillar) published with site go-live
- **Ongoing:** 1 article per week via AutoPublisher cron (Mon/Thu)
- **Sweep research:** Sunday + Wednesday, feeds AutoPublisher

## Affiliate Strategy

- Amazon Associates primary. No link dumping. Only products we use or would
  buy ourselves.
- Target 4–6 contextual affiliate links per article, woven into advice.
- Disclosure in site footer (already done). No inline disclosure blocks.
- All links: rel="noopener sponsored", target="_blank", proper Amazon tracking tag.
