Our Methods

How We Breed

Outdoor tubs and lined ponds — not sterile indoor racks. Our fish are raised in living water that cycles through plants and beneficial bacteria.

Outdoor Colonies

Fish live in planted tubs and ponds exposed to natural light, temperature swings, and live food. They're hardier and more colorful for it.

No Chemicals

We don't dose, treat, or medicate preventatively. If a fish needs perfect water to survive, it's not the right fish for our system.

Live Food Culture

Daphnia, moina, and infusoria cultures on-site. Fry get live food from day one — faster growth, better survival, stronger fish.
