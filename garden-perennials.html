Our Methods

How We Grow

Everything starts in living soil. No synthetic fertilizers, no growth regulators, no shortcuts. Plants that leave here are hardened off and ready for real conditions.

Living Soil Blocks

We use soil blocking instead of plastic cell trays. Better root structure, zero transplant shock, and no plastic waste.

Zone-Adapted Varieties

Everything we grow and sell is selected for our climate. These plants don't need to be babied.

Closed-Loop Fertility

Compost, worm castings, and compost tea — all made on-site from homestead inputs. Nothing leaves the system.
