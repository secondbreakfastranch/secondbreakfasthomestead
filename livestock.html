How We Raise Our Animals

No concrete. No confinement. Every animal here lives outdoors on rotated ground with fresh forage, clean water, and shelter from the elements.

Rotational Grazing

Pigs move through forest understory and pasture paddocks. They clear, till, and fertilize as they go — then the chickens follow.

No Growth Promoters

No antibiotics, no hormones, no synthetic growth promoters. Animals grow at their natural pace on a species-appropriate diet.

Local Processing

We work with a small, family-owned USDA processor. Animals are handled calmly and processed humanely, start to finish.
