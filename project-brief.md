# Second Breakfast Homestead — Project Brief

## Identity
- **Domain:** secondbreakfasthomestead.com
- **Registrar:** Porkbun
- **Parent:** Second Breakfast Ranch LLC
- **Tagline (primary):** "From Pasture, Pond & Garden"
- **Tagline (backup A):** "Livestock. Plants. Pond Life."
- **Tagline (backup B):** "Thoughtfully raised. Carefully grown. Sustainably kept."

## Three Pillars
1. **Livestock** — pasture/forest-raised pork, eggs, meat
2. **Plants** — starters, trees, pond plants, comfrey
3. **Aquatics** — ornamental fish (medaka, CPD, guppies), pond supplies, aquascaping

## Brand Voice
Confident, grounded, and warmly practical. A knowledgeable regenerative farmer who explains things clearly and directly, with quiet warmth. Professional enough to feel credible, but still approachable and human. No forced rural charm or folksy filler.

## Messaging Pillars
1. **Regenerative Practice** — building soil, closing loops, working with natural systems
2. **Integrated Systems** — livestock, plants, and water working together, not in isolation
3. **Practical Stewardship** — knowledge that's useful, actionable, and shared without pretense
4. **Family-Centered** — built for the long haul, with the next generation in view
5. **Transparent & Honest** — no marketing fluff, no exaggerated claims, just what works and what doesn't
